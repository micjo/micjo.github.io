Fosdem 2018

Fosdem 2018

Going to FOSDEM is always somewhat of a reality check. You see how much you stay in your silo at work, how few women there are in our sector and how small the campus has gotten over the last few years. Meeting old colleagues and learning new things always bring me back however.

There is never enough time to visit all the talks you want to go to. FOSDEM is a week worth of seminars condensed into 1 weekend.

Day 1

12.00 How to keep your embedded linux device up and running
12.45 Securing embedded systems using virtualization
13.30 Gstreamer for embedded devices
14.00 Modern tools to debug GStreamer applications
15.30 Piece of cake - testing remote embdded devices made easy
17.00 Igniting the open hardware ecosystem with RISC-V
18.30 Gstreamer for tiny devices

More and more speakers are (ab)using the term 'embedded' to lure in more attendants. Especially the talk on securing embedded devices was guilty of this

Day 2
